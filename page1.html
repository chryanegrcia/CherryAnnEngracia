Exercise Reflection

Creating a video presentation was both a challenging and rewarding experience that helped me develop my communication, technical, and time management skills. Throughout the process, I learned how to organize my thoughts clearly, use visual and audio elements effectively, and maintain audience engagement. Recording and editing the video also pushed me to become more comfortable speaking on camera and to pay closer attention to details such as pacing, tone, and clarity. While it required careful planning and multiple revisions, seeing the final product come together gave me a strong sense of accomplishment and showed me how powerful multimedia can be in delivering a message.
