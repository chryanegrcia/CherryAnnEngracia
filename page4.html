    ICE CREAM

    The track uses ice cream as a double-tendre, alluding to sexual acts and intimacy. The music video was released on the same day as the song – the 28th August 2020. The video shows Selena Gomez in an ice cream van, showing BLACKPINK surrounded by pastel buildings and lavish backgrounds.

Exercise Reflection

Practicing the use of lists, images, videos, and audio in HTML has deepened my understanding of how multimedia elements and structured content enhance user experience on a webpage. By working with lists, I learned how to organize information clearly using both ordered and unordered formats. Incorporating images taught me about the importance of file paths, alt text for accessibility, and responsive design. Embedding videos and audio files introduced me to HTML5 media tags, attributes like controls, autoplay, and preload, as well as the importance of supporting multiple file formats for compatibility. Overall, this practice has made me more confident in building engaging and interactive web pages.
